What is this?

Raising Fins is a campaign inspired by the Leave No Trace movement in the backpacking world.

The purpose is to promote a set of guidelines for sustainable and responsible SCUBA diving behavior.

The aim is to make the Oceans a cleaner, safer, and healthier place for all.

It is funded by the Millenium Campus Network's Millenium Ocean Prize, as part of the 9th Annual Millenium Campus Conference in Rabat, Morocco.
